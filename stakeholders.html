Recommendations

Policymakers

  • Create and enforce laws
  • Regulate industries and protect the public
  • Create clear regulations for AI transparency and accountability
  • Require disclosure when AI is used in content creation
  • Establish data protection laws for AI systems

Educators

  • Teach students and design curriculum
  • Evaluate learning and maintain academic standards
  • Redesign assignments to be AI-resistant (in-class work, oral exams)
  • Teach AI literacy (using tools responsibly)
  • Set clear policies on acceptable AI usage

Developers

  • Design, build, and maintain software systems
  • Develop algorithms and technological tools
  • Improve bias detection and mitigation
  • Add safeguards against harmful outputs
  • Increase transparency in model training

Business Leaders

  • Manage company operations and strategy
  • Make decisions about technology use
  • Develop ethical AI policies
  • Avoid over-reliance on AI decisions
  • Invest in human oversight systems

Everyday Users

  • Use AI in daily life
  • Participate as consumers and workers
  • Use AI as a tool, not a replacement for thinking
  • Verify information before trusting it
  • Avoid sharing sensitive data
