Technological Improvements

  • Improve fact-checking and source attribution: Integrate real-time retrieval systems so responses include verifiable citations.
  • Add transparency features: Include confidence indicators to show reliability of answers.
  • Reduce bias: Use diverse datasets and perform regular bias audits.
  • Strengthen safeguards: Limit responses in high-risk areas unless properly verified.

Social Strategies

  • Expand AI literacy: Teach users how AI works and its limitations.
  • Encourage critical thinking: Users should verify AI outputs.
  • Promote human oversight: AI should support—not replace—decision-making.
  • Establish ethical norms: Encourage transparency and responsible use.

Policy Recommendations

  • Require disclosure: Clearly label AI-generated content.
  • Regulate high-risk use: Apply stricter rules in healthcare, law, and politics.
  • Create accountability: Define responsibility for AI-related harm.
  • Promote global cooperation: Develop shared international AI standards.
